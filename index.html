ABOUT ME



Logan Ward is an Illustrator and recieved his Bachelor's degree in Illustration in May 2022. Logan spent the tail end of college focusing on a senior project in which he studied The New Yorker cartoons and created about 20 of his own. All of which have been submitted to The New Yorker and will not be shown on this website until he has heard back from the Magazine.

Logan continues to create cartoons and currently works part-time as an In-Residence Cartoonist at SecondCell Bio.

Illustrations are incredible tools for educating. Logan's goal is to make visuals that can promote social dialogue.
